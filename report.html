Report Incident Tutorial

This is the reporting form, which allows you to suggest areas of the city which you would like to put an air pollution sensor.
To identify a location suitable for a sensor, take a look for the following:

Idling cars

Exhaust from motor vehicles is a key component of ground-level ozone or smog, a contributor to climate change and causes serious health effects.

Industrial fires/waste incineration

Industrial fires such as tyre fires are harmful sources of air pollution that contain “volatile organic compounds” which are damaging to lungs, particularly in young children.

Dust and pollen

Dust (from artificial or natural sources) can be a big contributor to air pollution and effects human health.
